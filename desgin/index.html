frequently asked questions

how to cancel a reservation?

You can cancel your reservation by logging into your account or by contacting our customer service team at least 48 hours before your scheduled arrival.

are you currently hiring?

We are always looking for passionate people to join our team. Please visit our 'Careers' page to view all current job openings and to submit an application.

what payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, including Visa, MasterCard, and American Express. We also accept debit cards and mobile payments such as Apple Pay and Google Pay.

how can I redeem a coupon code?

You can redeem your coupon code during the online checkout process. Simply enter the code in the 'Promo Code' field before you finalize your payment.

what are the age requirements for booking?

Guests must be 21 years of age or older to book a room. Valid photo identification is required at check-in to confirm your age and identity.
